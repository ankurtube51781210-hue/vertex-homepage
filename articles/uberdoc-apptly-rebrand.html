UBERDOC Health rebrands to Apptly, signaling shift to direct-pay healthcare.

In a move to distinguish itself from ride-sharing associations, UBERDOC Health Technologies has officially rebranded to Apptly Health Technologies Corp.

The rebrand comes as the platform expands beyond simple specialist booking into a comprehensive direct-pay healthcare marketplace. By allowing patients to bypass insurance and pay cash directly for medical procedures, Apptly is capitalizing on the growing consumer frustration with hidden medical costs and opaque hospital billing practices.
