Abu Dhabi startup ecosystem surges 3,000% to $73.4 Billion.

The Middle East's push into the tech sector is yielding massive returns. According to the Global Startup Ecosystem Report 2026, Abu Dhabi has officially entered the world's top 50 emerging startup ecosystems.

Driven by aggressive government incentives and sovereign wealth investments into AI and Web3, the city's ecosystem value has surged by an unprecedented 3,000% over the last few years, reaching a valuation of $73.4 billion. Founders point to the zero-tax environment and rapid visa processing as key attractors for global talent.
