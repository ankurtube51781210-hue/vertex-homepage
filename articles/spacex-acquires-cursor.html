SpaceX acquires AI coding assistant Cursor in historic $60B deal.

In a landmark deal that merges aerospace engineering with artificial intelligence, SpaceX has officially signed an agreement to acquire the AI coding assistant startup Cursor for $60 billion.

This all-stock transaction ranks among the largest acquisitions of a venture-backed startup in global history. Following the announcement, SpaceX’s market capitalization surged past Amazon and Microsoft. Analysts suggest SpaceX intends to integrate Cursor's underlying models into its autonomous navigation systems and Starlink network infrastructure.
