TotalEnergies deploys 13,000 sensors for global AI Methane monitoring.

TotalEnergies has launched 'MethaneLive', an ambitious global monitoring center powered by artificial intelligence and an array of 13,000 physical sensors deployed across its worldwide infrastructure.

Showcased at the VivaTech summit, the system uses machine learning algorithms to instantly detect, pinpoint, and quantify methane leaks faster than traditional manual inspections. This deployment represents one of the largest industrial applications of 'edge AI' in the global push toward aggressive emissions reduction targets.
