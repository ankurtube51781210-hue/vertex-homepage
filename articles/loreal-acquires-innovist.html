L'Oréal acquires majority stake in science-led beauty startup Innovist.

In a major strategic move, L'Oréal has announced the acquisition of a majority stake in Innovist, the Indian digital-first personal care company behind popular science-backed brands like Bare Anatomy and Chemist at Play.

The acquisition highlights the beauty industry's increasing reliance on highly targeted, active-ingredient formulations tailored to regional demographics. Industry analysts estimate the deal values the fast-growing startup significantly higher than its most recent funding rounds.
