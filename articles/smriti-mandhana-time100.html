Smriti Mandhana named in TIME100 Most Influential People in Sports.

Indian women’s cricket vice-captain Smriti Mandhana has been honored with a spot on the prestigious TIME100 Most Influential People in Sports list for 2026. She is remarkably the only Indian athlete to be featured in this year's rankings.

Mandhana was recognized not just for her elegant batting and match-winning performances, but for her role in popularizing women's cricket globally and serving as a powerful advocate for equal pay in sports.
