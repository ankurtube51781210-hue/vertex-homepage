Lionel Messi ties all-time men's World Cup scoring record.

In a match that will be remembered for decades, Lionel Messi scored a brilliant hat-trick in Argentina’s 3-0 victory over Algeria during the group stages of the 2026 FIFA World Cup.

With these three goals, Messi has officially tied the all-time men’s World Cup scoring record, a milestone previously thought untouchable. Despite questions about his age and stamina entering the tournament, the Argentine captain proved once again that his technical brilliance and vision remain unmatched on the global stage.
