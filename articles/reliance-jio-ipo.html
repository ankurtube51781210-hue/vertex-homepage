Reliance Jio prepares for massive $4 Billion IPO filing.

According to market insiders, Reliance Jio is in the final stages of preparing its draft IPO papers for a massive $4 billion public issue. The filing is expected ahead of the company's Annual General Meeting scheduled for this Friday.

Jio's public debut has been highly anticipated by global investors, as the company has effectively monopolized India's 5G rollout and is rapidly expanding into AI data centers and digital infrastructure. This IPO could set a new benchmark for Asian tech valuations in 2026.
