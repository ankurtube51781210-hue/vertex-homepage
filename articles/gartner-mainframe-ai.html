Gartner: 70% of Mainframe Exits failing due to 'GenAI Delusion'.

A stark new report from Gartner warns that over 70% of enterprise mainframe migration projects initiated in 2026 are on track to fail. The primary culprit? The 'GenAI Delusion.'

Executives have increasingly relied on generative AI tools to automatically translate millions of lines of decades-old COBOL into modern languages like Java and Python. However, Gartner notes that the AI often introduces subtle, catastrophic logic errors in complex financial and operational systems, forcing companies to halt migrations and revert to expensive manual code reviews.
