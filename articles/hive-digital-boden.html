HIVE Digital acquires massive Swedish data center for Sovereign AI Compute.

HIVE Digital Technologies continues its aggressive pivot from cryptocurrency mining to artificial intelligence infrastructure by acquiring the 32 MW Big Boden data center in Sweden.

Previously a tenant, HIVE's outright purchase of the facility is part of a broader strategy to establish 'sovereign AI compute'—providing massive, localized GPU processing power for European governments and enterprises concerned with data privacy and cross-border data transfer regulations.
