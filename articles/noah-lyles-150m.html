Noah Lyles clocks jaw-dropping 14.67 seconds in the 150m sprint.

American track star Noah Lyles delivered a sensational performance at the Golden Spike meet, winning the rarely-run 150-meter sprint with a world-leading time of 14.67 seconds.

Lyles, who has dominated the 200m circuit, showcased explosive top-end speed that suggests he is in peak physical condition heading into the summer championships. The time establishes him as the undisputed favorite across both short sprint disciplines.
