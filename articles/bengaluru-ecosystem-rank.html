Bengaluru ranks 15th globally in 2026 Startup Ecosystem Report.

The Global Startup Ecosystem Report 2026 has officially ranked Bengaluru-Karnataka as the 15th best startup ecosystem in the world, and 4th overall in Asia. The city's total ecosystem value has now reached a staggering $152.8 billion.

The report highlighted Bengaluru's dominance in deep-tech, AI engineering talent, and SaaS exports as primary drivers of its continued growth. Despite global venture capital slowdowns, early-stage funding in the region remained highly resilient throughout the year.
