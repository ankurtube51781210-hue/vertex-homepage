The 'AI Efficiency' narrative: A silver bullet excuse for tech layoffs?

Despite posting record-breaking quarterly profits, the tech sector is currently experiencing a brutal wave of layoffs, tracking roughly 44% higher than the previous year. The primary reason cited by CEOs across Silicon Valley? 'AI Efficiency.'

However, a growing number of market analysts argue that AI is simply being used as a convenient 'silver bullet' excuse. By blaming AI restructuring, executives can justify massive workforce reductions to correct pandemic-era over-hiring, all while signaling aggressive forward-thinking growth to Wall Street investors. The reality of AI's impact on developer productivity remains highly debated internally.
