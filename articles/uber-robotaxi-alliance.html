Stellantis, Wayve, and Uber form global Robotaxi alliance.

The race for autonomous ride-hailing has just accelerated. Stellantis, AI-driving startup Wayve, and Uber have announced a massive trilateral collaboration to develop and deploy Level 4 driverless robotaxis globally.

The partnership combines Stellantis’ massive vehicle manufacturing platforms, Wayve’s next-generation 'embodied AI' driving technology, and Uber’s unparalleled mobility network. Testing will begin in designated European cities later this year, with full commercial rollout anticipated by early 2027.
